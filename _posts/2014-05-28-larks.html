Interactive Theater

I was invited by The Larks to collaborate on a game as part of their latest performance. The game was to include technology to reveal a narrative and challenge players.

Process

I worked closely with Jared Hemmings to build the required components, including: pressure-sensitive chairs, remote controls and a logic-fitted rotary phone.

We used Arduino to give our components logic and worked with The Larks to sync each prop component to video, music and lighting.

This was a one week collaboration resulting in a days worth of performances.
